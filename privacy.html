At AI Voinix, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

We collect personal information to enhance your experience with our apps. Cookies are used to simplify your experience and personalize content based on your preferences. By using our services, you consent to the use of cookies to collect and store data. You may adjust your browser settings to control cookie usage.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in specific circumstances, such as when you provide explicit consent or when required by law. We ensure that any shared information is protected with the same security measures that we use.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, and delete your personal information. You may also revoke permissions granted to our apps at any time. For further assistance, you can contact us via email at foinicks@outlook.com.

7. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Privacy Rights for California Residents

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, the right to request deletion of personal information, and the right to opt-out of data sharing for advertising purposes.

9. Privacy Rights for EU Users

Users in the European Union have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access your data, request deletion, restrict processing, and raise concerns with relevant data protection authorities.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated to you via in-app notifications or email. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
